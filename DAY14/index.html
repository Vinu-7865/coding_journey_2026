How it works

Learn it once. Review it forever.

1

Add what you learned

After each study session, paste your notes, key concepts, or code snippets into REF. Takes under a minute.

2

REF schedules reviews

The spaced repetition algorithm automatically decides when to remind you — 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, then monthly.

3

Rate your recall

Each review takes 2 minutes. Rate how well you remembered. REF adjusts the next review date based on your answer.
